# Specimen Policy: Global Asset Shield (IAPL)
**Form Number:** GAS-CB-2025-SPEC
**Carrier:** Carrier B
**Form Type:** Proprietary Form

## 1. COVERAGE PROVISIONS

### 1.1 Professional Services Liability
The Underwriter shall pay on behalf of any Insured all Loss resulting from a Claim first made during the Policy Period for a Wrongful Act. 

### 1.2 Regulatory Response Coverage (Proprietary)
The Underwriter shall pay Crisis Costs and Regulatory Defense Costs incurred by the Insured Entity in responding to a Formal Request for Information (RFI) or a Supervisory Examination by a Global Regulator (including but not limited to SEC, FCA, and ASIC).
*   **Sublimit:** $250,000 per Examination.
*   **Retention:** $0 for the first 100 hours of legal consulting.

### 1.3 Subpoena Defense
The Underwriter will pay legal fees incurred in responding to a third-party subpoena for documents or testimony served on an Insured Person, provided the subpoena relates to the rendering of Professional Services.

## 2. DEFINITIONS

**“Wrongful Act”** means:
(a) a negligent act, error, or omission;
(b) a breach of fiduciary duty under the Investment Advisers Act; or
(c) a "Privacy Wrongful Act" involving the accidental disclosure of non-public financial information.

**“Professional Services”** means the management of client assets, the provision of financial advice, and services as a "Solicitor" or "Placement Agent" if done in compliance with SEC Rule 206(4)-3. Excludes services related to the sale of insurance products.

**“Insured”** includes:
(a) The Wealth Management Firm (the Named Insured);
(b) Any Subsidiary;
(c) Any "Associated Natural Person" as defined by the SEC; and
(d) Any Former Officer, but only for acts committed during their tenure.

## 3. EXCLUSIONS

### 3.1 Unreasonable Profits
Excludes any Loss arising from the receipt of any commission or fee to which the Insured was not entitled. Carve-back: This exclusion does not apply to Defense Costs associated with "Fee Disputes" if the dispute is settled for less than 50% of the original fee.

### 3.2 Venture Capital and Private Equity
No coverage for any Wrongful Act committed in the management of a Private Equity Fund or Venture Capital Fund. (Note: Use Form GAS-PEX for such coverage).

### 3.3 Cyber/Ransomware
Absolute exclusion for any payments made as "Ransom" or for the recovery of data following a ransomware attack.

## 4. ENDORSEMENTS

**Endorsement GAS-CB-15: Mutual Fund Wrap**
Amends Section 3.2 to include coverage for the "Global Growth 40 Act Fund" as a covered Client.

**Endorsement GAS-CB-22: Independent Contractor Extension**
Adds 15 specified Independent Contractors to the definition of Insured Person.
