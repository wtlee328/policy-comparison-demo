# Specimen Policy: Alpha Advisor Comprehensive (IAPL)
**Form Number:** PROP-ALPHA-2025-V1
**Carrier:** Carrier A
**Form Type:** Proprietary Form

## I. INSURING AGREEMENTS

### (A) Professional Liability Coverage
The Insurer will pay on behalf of the Insureds all Loss from any Claim for a Wrongful Act in the performance of Professional Services. Coverage triggers upon delivery of a written Claim or a Civil Investigative Demand (CID).

### (B) Trade Error Correction (Cost of Correction)
The Insurer will reimburse the Company for Error Costs incurred by the Company to correct a Trade Error that would otherwise result in a Claim for a Wrongful Act. 
*   **Trigger:** The Trade Error must be discovered by the Insured during the Policy Period and reported to the Insurer within 10 business days.
*   **Condition:** No coverage is provided if the Trade Error is corrected after a Claim has been formally made by the affected Client.

### (C) Regulatory Inquiry Support
The Insurer will pay Investigation Costs incurred by an Insured Person in responding to a Formal Investigation or SEC sweep, even in the absence of an allegation of a Wrongful Act. 

## II. DEFINITIONS

**“Professional Services”** means:
(1) Investment management, asset allocation, and advisory services;
(2) Publishing of newsletters or research reports;
(3) The delivery of educational seminars or webinars; and
(4) Financial planning services if performed in conjunction with investment management.

**“Trade Error”** means an unintentional clerical or data entry error in the execution of a trade, including the purchase of an incorrect security, the wrong quantity of a security, or a trade executed for the wrong client account.

**“Loss”** includes damages, settlements, judgments, Defense Costs, and Investigation Costs. Loss also includes "Regulatory Penalties" where such penalties are non-criminal and are permitted to be insured by local law.

## III. EXCLUSIONS

### (A) Active Fraud
The Insurer shall not be liable for Loss arising from any dishonest or fraudulent act. This exclusion is subject to a "Final Adjudication" requirement. Defense costs are advanced until the final determination of fraud.

### (B) Fund Management Excellence
No coverage is provided for Claims arising from the management of any Mutual Fund or ETF for which the Company serves as the sponsor or primary underwriter, unless such fund is specifically scheduled in Endorsement PROP-05.

### (C) Cyber and Privacy
Excludes all Loss arising from the unauthorized access to Client PII (Personally Identifiable Information). This exclusion does not apply if the privacy breach is a direct result of an error in the Provision of Investment Advice.

## IV. PROPRIETARY ENDORSEMENTS

**Endorsement PROP-ALPHA-01: Soft Dollar Extension**
Provides a $100,000 sublimit for Claims arising from the alleged misuse of "Soft Dollar" arrangements or improper allocation of deal expenses.

**Endorsement PROP-ALPHA-09: Continuity of Coverage**
The "Prior and Pending" litigation date is waived for any Insured who has maintained continuous coverage with Carrier A for at least 5 consecutive years.
