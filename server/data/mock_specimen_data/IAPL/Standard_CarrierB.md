# Specimen Policy: Investment Advisory Liability (IAL)
**Form Number:** CB-IAL-2024-V4
**Carrier:** Carrier B
**Form Type:** Industry Standard

## SECTION I. COVERAGE GRANTS

### 1.1 Management and Professional Liability
The Underwriter will pay on behalf of the Insureds all Loss resulting from a Professional Claim first made against an Insured during the Policy Period for an Advisory Act.

### 1.2 Defense and Advancement
The Underwriter has the right and duty to defend any Professional Claim, even if the allegations are groundless. Defense Costs shall be paid by the Underwriter as part of the Loss and shall erode the Limit of Liability. The Underwriter shall advance covered Defense Costs on a current basis, but no later than 60 days after receipt of itemized invoices.

### 1.3 Allocation
If a Claim includes both covered and non-covered matters, or is made against both covered and non-covered parties, the Insureds and the Underwriter agree to use their best efforts to determine a fair and reasonable allocation between covered and non-covered Loss. In the absence of an agreement, the Underwriter shall advance Defense Costs on a 100% basis until a final allocation is reached.

## SECTION II. KEY DEFINITIONS

**“Advisory Act”** means any actual or alleged act, error, omission, misstatement, misleading statement, or breach of fiduciary duty committed solely in the rendering of Investment Advisory Services.

**“Investment Advisory Services”** means services performed for a Client of the Insured Entity for a fee or other compensation, consisting of investment management, financial planning, or investment advice. This definition includes services as a "Robo-Adviser" or automated platform if disclosed in the Application.

**“Insureds”** means (i) the Insured Entity, and (ii) any Insured Person.

**“Insured Person”** means any natural person who was, is, or shall be a director, officer, member, partner, or employee of the Insured Entity. Insured Person also includes any "leased employee" while performing duties for the Insured Entity under a written contract.

**“Loss”** means the total amount the Insureds become legally obligated to pay on account of a Claim, including settlements, judgments, and Defense Costs. Loss specifically includes punitive or exemplary damages where insurable under the law most favorable to the Insured.

## SECTION III. EXCLUSIONS

The Underwriter shall not be liable for Loss under this Policy on account of any Claim:

### 3.1 Dishonesty and Illegal Advantage
Based upon or arising out of any deliberately dishonest or fraudulent act, or the gaining of any profit to which the Insured was not legally entitled. This exclusion only applies if a final judgment or adjudication in a proceeding adverse to the Insured establishes such conduct. This exclusion shall not be imputed to any other natural person Insured.

### 3.2 Prior Notice
Based upon or arising out of any Advisory Act or circumstance which has been the subject of any notice given under any other policy of which this Policy is a renewal or replacement.

### 3.3 Contractual Liability
Based upon or arising out of any liability assumed by an Insured under any contract or agreement, provided that this exclusion shall not apply to liability the Insured would have in the absence of such contract.

## SECTION IV. ENDORSEMENTS

**Endorsement CB-V4-08: Cost of Correction (Limited)**
The Underwriter will reimburse the Insured Entity for a Correction Loss incurred to rectify a Trade Error, provided the Trade Error is discovered and reported within 72 hours of its occurrence. Sublimit: $50,000.

**Endorsement CB-V4-12: Amended Insured vs Insured**
Section 3.4 (Insured vs Insured) is deleted in its entirety and replaced with a carve-back for derivative suits brought without the assistance or solicitation of any Insured Person.
