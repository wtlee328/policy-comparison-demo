# Specimen Policy: Equity Prime Private Equity Form (PEX)
**Form Number:** EP-CA-2025-V1
**Carrier:** Carrier A
**Form Type:** Proprietary Form

## I. INSURING AGREEMENTS

### (A) Comprehensive Private Equity Liability
The Insurer will pay on behalf of the Insureds all Loss resulting from any Claim for a Wrongful Act. This includes "Portfolio Mismanagement" and "Improper Fund Allocation" between Limited Partners.

### (B) Crisis Intervention Support (Proprietary)
The Insurer will pay up to $100,000 for "Emergency Management Costs" incurred when the Private Equity Firm sends an Insured Person to temporarily manage a Portfolio Company following a "Crisis Event" (e.g., the sudden death of the PC’s CEO).

### (C) Secondary Transaction Coverage
The Insurer will pay Loss arising from a Claim made by a buyer or seller of a Limited Partner interest, alleging a Wrongful Act in the Firm’s facilitation of the "Secondary Market" transaction.

## II. DEFINITIONS

**“Portfolio Company”** is expanded to include "Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs)" and "Co-investment Vehicles" managed by the Private Equity Firm.

**“Wrongful Act”** includes:
(1) Failure to perform due diligence on a prospective investment;
(2) Error in the valuation of Fund assets;
(3) Breach of "Exclusivity" or "No-Shop" agreements during a deal.

**“Loss”** includes damages, settlements, Defense Costs, and "Reputational Harm Mitigation Costs" (sublimited).

## III. EXCLUSIONS

### (A) Intellectual Property
Excludes any Claim for patent, trademark, or copyright infringement. **Carve-back:** This exclusion does not apply to such claims if they are part of a broader Securities Claim.

### (B) Fund Waterfall Disputes
No coverage for Claims arising from the calculation or timing of "Carried Interest" or "Clawback" distributions to Partners. **Exception:** Defense Costs are provided for such claims.

### (C) Public Offering exclusion (POSI)
Excludes liability related to an Initial Public Offering (IPO) of a Portfolio Company. (A separate Public Offering Securities Insurance policy is required).

## IV. PROPRIETARY ENDORSEMENTS

**Endorsement EP-002: ESG Audit Defense**
Provides a $250,000 sublimit for Defense Costs incurred in responding to a formal SEC or regulatory audit of the Firm’s "ESG" (Environmental, Social, and Governance) disclosures.

**Endorsement EP-015: Broadened OEL Selection**
Allows the Firm to add "Outside Entities" to the OEL schedule mid-term without prior notice, provided the investment size is <10% of the Firm's total AUM.
