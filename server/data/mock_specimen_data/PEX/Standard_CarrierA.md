# Specimen Policy: Private Equity Management Liability (PEX)
**Form Number:** MCA-PEX-2024-V1
**Carrier:** Carrier A
**Form Type:** Industry Standard

## I. COVERAGE GRANTS

### (A) Management Liability
The Insurer shall pay, on behalf of the Insureds, Loss resulting from any Claim for a Wrongful Act in the management of the Private Equity Firm, but only when such Claim is first made during the Policy Period.

### (B) Outside Entity Liability (OEL)
The Insurer shall pay, on behalf of the Insured Persons, Loss resulting from a Claim for a Wrongful Act committed while serving at the written request of the Private Equity Firm as a director, officer, or manager of a Portfolio Company. 
*   **Provision:** This coverage is excess of any indemnification or insurance provided by the Portfolio Company.
*   **Sub-clause:** Coverage is limited to those Portfolio Companies disclosed in the Application and Scheduled via Endorsement.

### (C) Defense Costs and Advancement
The Insurer has the right and duty to defend any Claim. Defense Costs shall be advanced by the Insurer every 30 days upon receipt of reasonable invoices. Advancement is subject to an undertaking to repay if the Loss is ultimately determined to be non-covered.

## II. DEFINITIONS

**“Portfolio Company”** means any entity in which the Private Equity Firm, directly or indirectly, holds an equity investment (whether majority or minority).

**“Insured”** means the Private Equity Firm, any subsidiary, and any Insured Person.

**“Insured Person”** means:
(1) any General Partner, Managing Member, or Principal of the Private Equity Firm;
(2) any Employee of the Private Equity Firm while acting in a managerial capacity;
(3) any "Outside Director" serving on the board of a Portfolio Company at the Firm’s request.

**“Wrongful Act”** means any actual or alleged act, error, omission, or breach of duty committed in the course of (i) Venture Capital or Private Equity activities, or (ii) Investment Advisory Services.

## III. EXCLUSIONS

### (A) Illegal Conduct
Excludes any Loss arising from a deliberately fraudulent act. This exclusion applies only if established by a "Final, Non-Appealable Adjudication" in the underlying proceeding.

### (B) Professional Services Carve-out
Excludes liability arising from services as a broker, dealer, or investment banker. **Carve-back:** Does not apply to the Firm’s activities in identifying or negotiating the acquisition of Portfolio Companies.

### (C) Bodily Injury and Property Damage
Absolute exclusion for physical harm. **Exception:** This exclusion does not apply to a Securities Claim resulting from a catastrophic event at a Portfolio Company.

## IV. ENDORSEMENTS

**Endorsement PEX-MCA-01: Run-off for Retired Partners**
Provides an automatic 6-year Discovery Period for any Partner who retires during the Policy Period, specifically for acts committed prior to their retirement date.

**Endorsement PEX-MCA-09: Amended OEL Priority**
Specifies that this Policy is primary to any personal umbrella liability policy held by an Insured Person, but excess to any insurance held by the Portfolio Company.
