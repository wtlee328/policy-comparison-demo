# Specimen Policy: LP Guardian Shield (PEX)
**Form Number:** LPGS-CB-2025
**Carrier:** Carrier B
**Form Type:** Proprietary Form

## SECTION 1. INSURING CLAUSES

### 1.1 Limited Partner Dispute Mitigation (Proprietary)
The Underwriter will pay up to $50,000 for "Mediation Expenses" incurred to resolve a dispute with one or more Limited Partners (LPs) before such dispute becomes a formal Claim.
*   **Trigger:** Written notice from an LP of their intent to sue or a "books and records" request under state law.

### 1.2 Co-Investment Network Liability
The Underwriter will pay Loss on behalf of "Co-Investors" who invest alongside the main Private Equity Fund, provided such Co-Investors are acting solely as passive investors and have no managerial control.

### 1.3 Successor Liability Protection
Coverage is extended to any liability of a "Predecessor Management Firm" acquired by the Named Insured, provided such liability arises from acts committed after the "Retroactive Date."

## SECTION 2. DEFINITIONS

**“Wrongful Act”** means:
(i) any breach of fiduciary duty;
(ii) any "Allocation Error" involving the charging of deal expenses to the Fund instead of the Firm;
(iii) an "Unintentional" violation of the Investment Advisers Act.

**“Insured”** includes:
(a) The Private Equity Firm and all managed Funds;
(b) All "General Partners" (individuals and entities);
(c) "Venture Partners" and "Operating Partners."

**“Loss”** includes damages, settlements, and Defense Costs. It also includes "LP Communication Expenses" incurred to explain a significant Fund loss to investors.

## SECTION 3. EXCLUSIONS

### 3.1 Product Liability (Portfolio Company)
Absolute exclusion for any Claim arising from the design, manufacture, or sale of any product by a Portfolio Company.

### 3.2 ERISA / Pension Mismanagement
Standard exclusion for violations of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act.

### 3.3 Prior Knowledge (The "Knowledge Date")
Excludes any Claim based upon any fact or circumstance known to the "Senior Management" as of the Knowledge Date (January 1, 2023).

## SECTION 4. ENDORSEMENTS

**Endorsement LPGS-12: Broad Regulatory Coverage (SEC/DOJ)**
Deletes the exclusion for "Fines and Penalties" as it relates to civil fines imposed by the SEC, to the extent such fines are insurable by law.

**Endorsement LPGS-18: Key Person Transition Extension**
Adds a $75,000 sublimit for legal and accounting fees associated with restructuring the GP entity following the departure of a Key Person.
