# Specimen Policy: Investment Fund Liability (IFL)
**Form Number:** MCA-IFL-2024-V2
**Carrier:** Carrier A
**Form Type:** Industry Standard

## I. INSURING AGREEMENTS

### (A) Side A: Individual Non-Indemnified Liability
The Insurer shall pay, on behalf of each Individual Insured, Loss resulting from any Claim first made against such Individual Insured during the Policy Period for a Wrongful Act, but only to the extent that the Fund is not required or permitted to indemnify the Individual Insured. **Retention:** $0.

### (B) Side B: Fund Indemnification
The Insurer shall pay, on behalf of the Fund, Loss resulting from any Claim first made against an Individual Insured during the Policy Period for a Wrongful Act, but only to the extent that the Fund has indemnified such Individual Insured for such Loss.

### (C) Side C: Entity Securities Liability
The Insurer shall pay, on behalf of the Fund, Loss resulting from any Securities Claim first made against the Fund during the Policy Period for a Wrongful Act by the Fund.

### (D) Defense and Advancement
The Insurer has the right and duty to defend any Claim seeking covered Loss. The Insurer shall advance Defense Costs on a current basis, subject to a written undertaking from the Insured to repay such costs if it is finally determined that the Loss is not covered.

## II. DEFINITIONS

**“Individual Insured”** means any natural person who was, is, or shall be:
(1) a Director, Officer, Manager, or equivalent of the Fund or a Subsidiary;
(2) a member of a Fund’s advisory board or investment committee; or
(3) an Employee of the Fund, but only for a Securities Claim or while acting in a managerial capacity.

**“Securities Claim”** means a Claim made against any Insured alleging a violation of the Securities Act of 1933, the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, or any state "Blue Sky" law. Securities Claim does not include any Claim brought by a vendor or competitor of the Fund unless such Claim is also brought by a shareholder in their capacity as such.

**“Wrongful Act”** means:
(1) with respect to an Individual Insured, any actual or alleged act, error, omission, neglect, or breach of duty;
(2) with respect to the Fund, any actual or alleged act, error, or omission in connection with a Securities Claim.

**“Loss”** includes judgments, settlements, and Defense Costs. Loss also includes "Stockholders' Derivative Demand Investigation Costs" up to a sublimit of $100,000.

## III. EXCLUSIONS

### (A) Insured vs. Insured (Standard)
The Insurer shall not be liable for Loss on account of any Claim brought by or on behalf of any Insured. **Carve-back:** This exclusion does not apply to:
(i) any Claim brought in a jurisdiction outside the United States;
(ii) any derivative suit brought independently of any Insured; or
(iii) any Claim brought by a former Individual Insured who has not served the Fund for at least 3 years.

### (B) Short-Swing Profits
Based upon or arising out of any violation of Section 16(b) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (Short-Swing Profits).

### (C) Prior Acts
Based upon any Wrongful Act occurring before the "Prior Acts Date" of January 1, 2022.

## IV. ENDORSEMENTS

**Endorsement IFL-MCA-05: Amended Pollution Exclusion**
Narrows the exclusion to apply only to the discharge of hazardous waste into the environment, providing a carve-back for derivative suits following a pollution event.

**Endorsement IFL-MCA-12: Spousal Extension**
Extends the definition of Individual Insured to include the lawful spouse or domestic partner of an Individual Insured for Claims seeking Loss from marital property.
