# Specimen Policy: Management and Fund Liability (MFL)
**Form Number:** CB-MFL-2024-V1
**Carrier:** Carrier B
**Form Type:** Industry Standard

## SECTION I. INSURING CLAUSES

### 1.1 Management Liability
The Underwriter will pay on behalf of the Insured Persons all Loss for which the Insured Persons are not indemnified by the Fund and which they are legally obligated to pay on account of a Claim for a Wrongful Act.

### 1.2 Fund Reimbursement
The Underwriter will pay on behalf of the Fund all Loss for which the Fund has indemnified the Insured Persons on account of a Claim for a Wrongful Act.

### 1.3 Entity Liability (Fund)
The Underwriter will pay on behalf of the Fund all Loss which the Fund is legally obligated to pay on account of a Claim first made against the Fund for a Wrongful Act by the Fund.

### 1.4 Defense and Settlement
The Underwriter shall have the right and duty to defend any Claim even if false or fraudulent. The Underwriter may settle any Claim if it is in the best interest of the Fund, provided the Fund’s consent is obtained (such consent not to be unreasonably withheld).

## SECTION II. DEFINITIONS

**“Fund”** means the entity named in the Declarations and any Subsidiary.

**“Insured Persons”** means all natural persons who were, are, or shall be duly elected or appointed directors, officers, or trustees of the Fund, or their functional equivalent. Coverage also extends to "General Counsel" and "Compliance Officers."

**“Wrongful Act”** means any actual or alleged act, error, omission, misstatement, misleading statement, or breach of duty committed by an Insured Person in their capacity as such, or by the Fund.

**“Loss”** includes compensatory damages, statutory multiples, and Defense Costs. Loss does not include (a) taxes, (b) fines or penalties, or (c) the multiple portion of multiplied damages.

## SECTION III. EXCLUSIONS

### 3.1 Fraud and Personal Profit
The Underwriter shall not be liable for Loss arising from any Insured’s fraudulent or criminal act or the gaining of any profit to which the Insured was not legally entitled. This exclusion only applies if established by a "Final Adjudication" in the underlying action. 

### 3.2 Pending and Prior Litigation
No coverage for any Claim based upon any litigation or proceeding pending against any Insured as of the "Pending and Prior Date."

### 3.3 Bodily Injury / Property Damage
Excludes all liability for physical injury to persons or damage to tangible property. **Carve-back:** This exclusion does not apply to "Non-Indemnified Side A" Defense Costs in an action alleging such injury.

## SECTION IV. ENDORSEMENTS

**Endorsement CB-MFL-04: Continuity Amendment**
Limits the "Prior Knowledge" application to the Knowledge of the Chief Executive Officer and Chief Financial Officer only.

**Endorsement CB-MFL-09: Derivative Demand Investigation**
Adds a $50,000 sublimit for reasonable legal fees incurred by the Fund in evaluating a demand by a shareholder to bring a lawsuit.
