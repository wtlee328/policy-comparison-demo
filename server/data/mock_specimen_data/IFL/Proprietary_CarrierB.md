# Specimen Policy: Secure Fund 360 (IFL)
**Form Number:** SF360-CB-2025
**Carrier:** Carrier B
**Form Type:** Proprietary Form

## SECTION 1. THE INSURING CLAUSES

### 1.1 Fund Management Liability
The Underwriter shall pay on behalf of the Insureds all Loss resulting from a Claim for a Wrongful Act. This includes coverage for "Soft Dollar" violations and "Inter-Fund" transaction disputes.

### 1.2 Key Person Event Benefit (Proprietary)
The Underwriter will reimburse the Fund up to $50,000 for reasonable costs (e.g., search firm fees) incurred to replace a "Key Person" following their death, disability, or permanent resignation. 
*   **Restriction:** This benefit does not cover the salary of the replacement person.

### 1.3 Subpoena and Witness Costs
The Underwriter will pay up to $25,000 for the legal representation of an Insured Person called as a witness in a proceeding where the Fund is not yet a party.

## SECTION 2. DEFINITIONS OF TERMS

**“Wrongful Act”** means:
(i) any breach of fiduciary duty;
(ii) any "Investment Error" including the failure to follow a Prospectus or PPM;
(iii) an "unintentional" violation of the Investment Company Act of 1940.

**“Insured”** includes:
(a) The Fund Family (all listed entities);
(b) The Investment Manager (only while managing the Fund);
(c) All "Independent Trustees."

**“Loss”** includes damages, settlements, and Defense Costs. Loss also includes "Corporate Reputation Costs" (sublimited).

## SECTION 3. EXCLUSIONS

### 3.1 Performance and Carried Interest
The Underwriter is not liable for any Claim based upon the calculation or distribution of "Carried Interest" or "Performance Fees." **Carve-back:** This exclusion does not apply to Defense Costs.

### 3.2 Professional Services Carve-out
No coverage for services as a real estate agent, lawyer, or accountant, unless such services are performed solely as part of the Fund's investment strategy.

### 3.3 Prior Knowledge
Excludes any Claim arising from any fact or circumstance disclosed in the "Prior Notice Letter" dated December 15, 2023.

## SECTION 4. ENDORSEMENTS

**Endorsement SF-360-14: Cyber Wrap (Limited)**
Adds a $100,000 sublimit for "Data Restoration Costs" following a malware infection of the Fund's registry.

**Endorsement SF-360-20: Broad Selection of Counsel**
Amends the "Defense" section to allow the Insured to select their own legal counsel from the "Carrier B Approved Panel" without prior consent.
