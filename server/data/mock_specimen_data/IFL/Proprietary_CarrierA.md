# Specimen Policy: Fund Guardian Elite (IFL)
**Form Number:** FG-CA-2025-V2
**Carrier:** Carrier A
**Form Type:** Proprietary Form

## I. INSURING AGREEMENTS

### (A) Portfolio Company Board Protection (Proprietary)
The Insurer shall pay Loss on behalf of any Insured Person arising from a Claim for a Wrongful Act committed while such Insured Person is serving at the written request of the Fund as a director, officer, or manager of an unaffiliated Portfolio Company (the "Outside Entity").
*   **Condition:** This coverage is "double-excess" over (1) any indemnification provided by the Outside Entity and (2) any D&O insurance maintained by the Outside Entity.

### (B) Investigative Order Response
The Insurer will pay Defense Costs incurred by an Individual Insured in responding to a Formal Investigative Order or Wells Notice. Coverage is provided regardless of whether a Wrongful Act is eventually alleged.

### (C) Zero-Deductible Side A
For any Loss where the Fund is financially unable to indemnify an Individual Insured (due to insolvency) or is legally prohibited from doing so, the Retention shall be $0.

## II. DEFINITIONS

**“Claim”** is expanded to include:
(a) A written request to toll the statute of limitations;
(b) A Search Warrant served on the Fund; and
(c) A "Notice of Assessment" from a foreign regulatory authority.

**“Insured Person”** includes:
(a) The Fund's Portfolio Managers;
(b) Risk Managers; and
(c) Advisory Board Members (specifically "Special Advisors").

**“Wrongful Act”** includes "Internal Allocation Errors" and "Improper Expense Offsets" between different funds in the same Fund Family.

## III. EXCLUSIONS

### (A) Final Adjudication Conduct
Excludes fraud or illegal profit only upon a final, non-appealable judgment by a court of competent jurisdiction. This exclusion will not apply if the case is settled or dismissed.

### (B) ERISA and Benefits
Standard exclusion for pension mismanagement. **Carve-back:** Does not apply to Claims for "Promissory Estoppel" related to the promise of fund shares as compensation.

### (C) Specific Fund Exclusions
No coverage is provided for the "Carrier A distressed debt fund" (managed by a separate subsidiary).

## IV. PROPRIETARY ENDORSEMENTS

**Endorsement FG-001: Automatic New Fund Coverage**
Any New Fund created by the Named Insured during the Policy Period is automatically covered for 120 days, provided its assets do not exceed $500,000,000.

**Endorsement FG-022: PR Crisis Management**
Provides a $75,000 sublimit for a "Crisis Event" defined as any negative media report that results in a withdrawal of >10% of the Fund's AUM in a 30-day period.
